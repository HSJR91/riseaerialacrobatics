Frequently Asked Questions

Not covered below? Just email or DM !

Do I need previous experience?

+
No! We welcome complete beginners. Our classes are structured to accommodate all levels, and our instructors will help you progress at your own pace. Just bring enthusiasm and an open mind.

What should I wear to class?

+
Wear comfortable, flexible clothing like leggings, joggers, or shorts paired with a t-shirt or tank top. Avoid loose clothing that could get caught. You'll be barefoot for aerial work, so no shoes needed! Bring a water bottle and a small towel.

Is it safe?

+
Yes, safety is our top priority. All equipment is professionally maintained and regularly inspected. Our instructors are certified and trained in techniques. We start with foundational skills and progress gradually.

How fit do I need to be?

+
Zero fitness required! You don't need to be fit to start! Aerial training builds your strength and fitness. We start with basics and exercises tailored to your fitness level. You'll be surprised at how quickly you progress.

How much do classes cost?

+
Monthly subscriptions range form £60 - £120 per month, no contract! Drop in classes start from £15. See Gymcatch for details.

Can I book private lessons?

+
Absolutely! Private sessions are available for personalized training. Contact team@rise-aerial.com to discuss your goals and arrange a custom package.
