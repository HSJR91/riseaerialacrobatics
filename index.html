Frequently Asked Questions About Aerial Classes

Not covered below? Just email us at team@rise-aerial.com or DM us on Instagram @rise_aerial_belfast.

Do I need previous aerial acrobatics or pole experience?

No! Rise Aerial welcomes complete beginners. Our beginner classes are specifically designed for people with no prior aerial arts experience. Our qualified instructors tailor their instruction to your level, whether you're brand new or have years of aerial training. We recommend starting with a beginner-level class to learn proper technique and safety fundamentals.

What should I wear to aerial acrobatics class?

Wear comfortable, fitted clothing such as leggings. Avoid loose clothing that can get caught on equipment. You'll be barefoot or in grip socks for better traction on the equipment.

Is aerial acrobatics safe?

Yes, aerial arts are safe when practiced with proper instruction, correct technique, and well-maintained equipment. Rise Aerial uses professionally maintained equipment, provides comprehensive safety training, and ensures all students learn proper progressions. Our qualified instructors prioritize safety in every class and never push students beyond their capability.

How fit do I need to be to start aerial classes?

You don't need to be fit to start! Aerial classes naturally build strength, flexibility, and cardiovascular fitness over time. Our beginner classes are designed for all fitness levels, and instructors provide modifications for every exercise. Many students find aerial arts more engaging and enjoyable than traditional gym workouts because of the fun, community-focused environment.

Can I book private lessons or custom group sessions?

Absolutely! We offer personalized 1:1 private lessons tailored to your goals and level, as well as custom group session packages for teams, friends, or special occasions. Contact us at team@rise-aerial.com with your requirements, and we'll create a personalized program for you.

What's included in my first aerial class?

Your first class includes a warm-up to prepare your body, equipment introduction and safety briefing, basic technique instruction on your chosen discipline, and a cool-down. Our instructors will assess your level and ensure you feel safe, supported, and celebrated. You'll leave with fundamental skills and a real sense of what the Rise Aerial community is all about.
